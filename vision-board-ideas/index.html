How to Choose the Right Images

Be specific: Instead of generic "luxury car," use the exact model and color you want.

Show the outcome: Not "studying hard" but "holding the diploma."

Include people: Images with people showing emotion are more powerful than objects alone.

Use personal photos: Mix aspirational images with photos of yourself, family, or places you love.

Add text: Powerful words, affirmations, or specific dates/numbers reinforce the visuals.
