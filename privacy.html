NoNoise

Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2024

Overview

NoNoise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the NoNoise macOS application.

Key Points

  • ✅ All your personal data stays on your Mac
  • ✅ No collection of habit names, milestone titles, or vision board content
  • ✅ Anonymous analytics only to improve the app
  • ✅ No advertising or data selling
  • ✅ You have full control of your data

Information Collection and Use

Data We Store Locally

NoNoise stores all your data locally on your device using UserDefaults. This includes:

  • Habits: Your habit titles, emojis, times, colors, and recurrence settings
  • Habit Completions: Records of when you marked habits as complete
  • Milestones: Your timeline milestones (dates, titles, and emojis)

Important: All data is stored locally on your Mac. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data.

Anonymous Analytics

To improve NoNoise and understand which features are most valuable to our users, we collect anonymous usage analytics using Mixpanel:

  • What we track: Which features you use (Timeline, Habits, Vision Board), app launches, and button interactions
  • Why we track it: To understand what our users value most and prioritize feature development
  • What we DON'T track: Your habit names, milestone titles, vision board content, or any personal information

Important: All analytics are anonymous and used solely to improve the app experience. We do not use this data for advertising, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your habit titles, milestone names, or vision board content
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Location data
  • Contact information or photos

Subscriptions and Payments

NoNoise Pro is available as a subscription or one-time purchase through Apple's In-App Purchase system:

  • All payments are processed by Apple
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Subscription status is managed by Apple
  • Apple's Privacy Policy applies to all payment processing

Your subscription information (status, expiration date) is provided by Apple's StoreKit framework and stored locally on your device.

Data Backup

Your data is stored locally using UserDefaults and will be included in:

  • Time Machine backups (if you use Time Machine)
  • iCloud backups (if you have macOS settings backed up to iCloud)

These backups are managed by macOS and Apple. We do not have access to your backed-up data.

Data Deletion

To delete all your NoNoise data:

  • Open NoNoise app → Click logo → Settings → Delete All Data
  • Or uninstall the NoNoise app from your Mac

All your local data will be permanently deleted when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

NoNoise does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect any information, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

App Store Compliance

NoNoise complies with:

  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • Apple's Privacy Requirements for macOS apps
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - as no personal data is collected
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) - as no personal data is collected

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data:

  • There is no data for us to access, modify, or delete
  • All your data is under your control on your device
  • You can export or delete your data at any time by managing the app files on your Mac

Technical Details

Data Storage

Data is stored using UserDefaults in Apple's Property List format within the app's sandboxed container. Your data is encrypted by macOS FileVault (if enabled).

App Sandbox

NoNoise runs in Apple's App Sandbox with limited permissions:

  • File access: Read/write to app container only
  • Network: Limited to subscription verification and anonymous analytics
  • Hardware: No camera, microphone, or location access

Third-Party Services

NoNoise uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit & RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Mixpanel: For anonymous usage analytics to improve the app

These services have their own privacy policies. Your personal data (habits, milestones, visions) is never shared with these services.
